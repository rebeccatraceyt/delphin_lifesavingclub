Club Ethos

Delphin Lifesaving Club is a multi-generational Swimming and Lifesaving Club, with over thirty years of service in Swimming and Lifesaving in Dublin.

Our ethos is equality and respect, within a fun learning envrionment.

  • Our instructors are fully qualified through the National Governing Body (NGB) for Swimming in Ireland and the Irish Branch of the Royal Lifesaving Society.
  • We deliver our classes in a safe and child-focused envrionment, teaching one of the most important life skills: How to stay safe around all waterways.
  • We provide a complete aquatic pathway, with swimmers progressing from learners to teachers and lifesavers progressing from basic first-aid training to fully-qualified Pool Lifeguards (you may even spot some of our past pupils on poolside!)

We look forward to welcoming you at the pool soon!
